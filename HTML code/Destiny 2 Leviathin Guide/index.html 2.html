For Titans, sentinel is best suited for a support role to increase your damage, strikers are good for clearing enemy’s but aren’t effective for boss damage phases other than the dogs, sun breakers deal the most damage and can apply debuffs to enemies. For Hunters, Nightstalker allows you to gain super energy back faster and combined with the Orpheus rig boots allows for faster super regeneration, Gunslinger with celestial nighthawk does the most damage of anything in the game, arcstriders are best suited for clearing enemies. For Warlocks, well of radiance is a must as it provides better healing and increased damage which makes the raid much easier, both voidwalker and stormcaller are best suited for clearing enemies however novabomb is effective for dealing huge damage or locking down areas.
